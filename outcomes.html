Pharmacy School: Outcomes

What Happens After Graduation

After graduation people wonder what happens. After graduating pharmacy school you have a large chance of having a job in place. First, you have to take your boards and pass in order to become an active pharmacist.

What jobs can you obtain?

With a PharmD degree you can get a multitude of jobs:

  • Pharmacist in both Retail Stores and a Hospital
  • Clinical Pharmacy
  • Pharmocoeconomics
  • Pharmacy Education
  • Managed Care
  • Specialized Area Oppurtunites: Residencies
  • Government Agencies

Salary

With a doctor of pharmacy degree, on average you make a 6 figure salary. Hourly wages range depending on what type of job you have choosen.Here are some examples of hourly wages:

  • Pharmacist: $47-$66
  • Clinical Pharmacist: $48-$65
  • Pharmacy Manager: $57-$70
