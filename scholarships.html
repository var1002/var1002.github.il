Pharmacy School: Scholarships

Scholarships

Pharmacy school just like any school is expensive. Most people can't afford to pay a full tuition out of pocket and have to take out loans. A way you can avoid loans is scholarships. Apply to as many scholarships as possible to reduce the amount you need to take out in loans but also pay out of pocket.

How can I get Scholarships?

One way of getting scholarships is seeing if your workplace especially if it's a pharmacy offers any scholarships. Another is through looking at the FASFA website and see if the offer any. Looking for scholarships can be a long process and require a million essays. One tip is to write a universal essay to use for all scholarship applications that have similar guidelines for the essay.
