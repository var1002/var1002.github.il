The Medical Field: Pharmacy

What is Pharmacy?

Pharmacy is the study of medicine. In this study you can learn how drugs are created, chemistry of medications, reactions, and interactions.

What degree do you need to get into Pharmacy School's?

Not all school's require having a bachelor's degree in order to obtain a doctorate in pharmacy. Some school's just require pre-requisites and offer programs within the school to obtain the degree. For example, UNH Manchester offers a program with MCPHS. This program is accelerated and includes doing 3 year's at UNHM and obtain a bachelor's in Biology. After 3 years at UNHM completing all the pre-requisites, you then do 3 years at MCPHS after completing the application process.

What school's have pharmacy programs?

There are many school's that offer pharmacy programs throughout the East Coast, below are some listed:
